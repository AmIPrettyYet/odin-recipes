Pikelet Recipe

Here's how to make yummy pikelets taking inspiration from RecipeTinEats.

Ingredients you'll need:

  • 185g plain flour
  • 2.5 Teaspoons of Baking Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons of White Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 185ml of Milk
  • 0.5 Teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Teaspoons of Butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk dry – Whisk the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add wet – Make a well in the centre. Add the egg, milk and vanilla, then whisk until it's almost lump free. The batter should be the consistency like thick honey – looser than tomato sauce, thicker than maple syrup.
  3. Melt 1 tsp butter in a non-stick pan over medium high heat (or medium, for strong stoves). Then most of the butter off with a paper towel so there are no visible drops of bubbles of butter.
  4. Drop ~2 tbsp batter into the pan and coax into 7.5cm/3" rounds. An ice cream scoop with lever will be your friend here. I cook 4 at a time.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface and once 4 or more of these bubbles pop (1 1/2 minutes), this means they are ready to flip. Flip with confidence! Then cook the other side for 1 minute or until golden.
  6. Keep cooking – Remove onto a plate. Cook the next batch (no need to add extra butter until 3rd batch).
  7. Serve warm with jam and cream, or butter!
